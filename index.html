Short Bio

My adventure began with a leap from Pretoria to Cape Town, where I initially studied travel and tourism. However, destiny had different plans for me. My passion for food ignited during my time in Cape Town, completely changing my path. My culinary journey truly began when I started working in a local restaurant. It was there that I crossed paths with the renowned chef, Casper, who took me under his wing. Casper's mentorship kindled a fire within me, and I soon found myself immersed in the world of flavors and techniques. Driven by a hunger for excellence, I relocated to Port Elizabeth to carve my own culinary path.

Determined and resourceful, I kicked off my journey by trading fresh seafood, gradually saving up for a dream that had taken root in my heart. After diligent planning and tireless effort, I realized my dream, the birth of "Oceans 11," my very own diner nestled along the harbor. A year later, it finally happened, and the doors of Oceans 11 swung open, inviting patrons to savor my culinary creations.

As time flowed, my passion continued to flourish. A decade down the line, not only did Oceans 11 thrive, but I also expanded my dreams into reality with the opening of two more branches. Beyond the kitchen, I find solace in the embrace of nature,fishing in tranquil waters and challenging myself on the golf course. Recently, a new passion has taken hold of me , the world of coding. Much like cooking, coding is an art of crafting, a symphony of logic and creativity that I've grown to adore.
